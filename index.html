Like Nowhere You've Ever Been

Welcome to our little town of Nowhereston, CT. We are a small coastal town that just opened our first hotel! Our Little Hotel is walking distance from John Rowland State Beach, and our Intercontinental Breakfast Buffet recently received 5 flapjacks from the Nowhereston Gazette. We hope you'll come visit us sometime soon and take in a quieter and quainter side of life. Plan Your Stay Today!

Rolling Down The River

If the beach isn't really your scene, you can take a canoe trip down Walter Becker River. Our local boat launch offers both canoes and kayaks. Ask about our Kidz Kanoes (limited quantities available)! Guided tours are also available, in which a local tour guide will show guests historical landmarks from our humble town.

Cruise Around In Style

Enjoy a scenic drive over the hills and dales of Nowhereston. Our local car rental office has a large inventory with a wide variety of vehicles. From Economy to Luxury, Uncle Jesse has it all!
