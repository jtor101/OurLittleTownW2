Welcome to our little town of Nowhereston, CT. We are a small coastal town that just opened our first hotel! Our Little Hotel is walking distance from John Rowland State Beach, and our Intercontinental Breakfast Buffet recently received 5 flapjacks from the Nowhereston Gazette. We hope you'll come visit us sometime soon and take in a quieter and quainter side of life.
If the beach isn't really your scene, you can take a canoe trip rolling down Walter Becker River. Our local boat launch offers both canoes and kayaks. Ask about our Kidz Kanoes (limited quantities available)! Guided tours are also available, in which a local tour guide will show guests historical landmarks from our humble town.
